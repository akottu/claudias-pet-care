Who We Are

Claudia's Pet Care is a trusted provider of professional pet sitting and dog walking services based in Wake Forest, NC. With years of experience and a passion for animals, we ensure your pets are cared for like family.

Having worked 7+ years in numerous Veterinary/Boarding Kennels, I understand that most pets are less stressed when cared for in their own familiar environment. That's why I chose to focus on providing high-quality in-home pet care services.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide reliable, loving, and personalized care for your pets, giving you peace of mind while you're away.

Benefits of In-Home Pet Sitting vs. Boarding Facilities

From my personal experience alone, having worked 7+ years in numerous Veterinary/Boarding Kennels, I would strongly advocate for most pets that in-home pet sitting is much less stressful than the kennel, boarding, or doggy daycare facility environments.

If this is an option you are considering or would like more insight on, please feel free to reach out and I would love to discuss more about your pet's personality, routines, and which care option would best fit their needs!
